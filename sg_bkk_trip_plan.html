04Culture & River Day

Riverside cluster — all three stops sit on or near the Chao Phraya, so transit is boat and short Grabs, not long hauls.

Wat Pho

8:00AM · RECLINING BUDDHA · 4.7★ (37.7k)
Do
Arrive at opening to beat both heat and tour groups. ~2hrs covers the reclining Buddha hall and the wider temple grounds. Entrance ~THB 300pp.
Dress
Shoulders and knees covered, strictly enforced.

Wat Arun

10:30AM · RIVERSIDE PRANG · 4.7★ (44.7k)
Do
Cross-river ferry from the pier near Wat Pho (a few minutes, cheap). The porcelain-inlaid prang photographs well any time of day; sunset is the famous shot but you won't be back this way in the evening, so late morning is the practical call.

ICONSIAM

2:00PM · RIVERSIDE MALL · 4.7★ (58.5k)
Do
Indoor floating market food hall, luxury and local retail, and a covered rain fallback if the afternoon downpour hits on schedule. Reachable by river-crossing boat/shuttle from the Wat Arun side — no need to go back through traffic.
Eat
Sook Siam (the indoor floating market) covers Thai regional food in one air-conditioned hall — good group option if appetites diverge.
