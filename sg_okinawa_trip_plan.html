03Dive Days — Kerama Islands

One hub, two visits. Kerama National Marine Park, ~1hr boat from Tomari Port, Naha — no domestic flight, no second base.

Kerama Islands — Full-Day Boat Dive

TOMARI PORT DEPARTURE · ~7:30–8AM · 2–3 DIVES
Operators (named, verify current rates)
Aloha Divers Okinawa, SEAKERS Okinawa, or GOLD Scuba Diving — all English-capable, Naha-based, run daily Kerama boat trips with hotel pickup. Full equipment rental (BCD, regulator, 5mm wetsuit, fins, computer) runs around ¥4,000/day per diver at market rate.
Conditions in March
Water ~19–22°C, visibility often 20–30m+. This is winter/early-spring water — a proper wetsuit is non-negotiable, not a preference.
Bonus
Humpback whale season runs Jan 12–Mar 31 in the Kerama channel — several operators fold whale watching into the boat transit at no extra cost. Not guaranteed, weather- and luck-dependent.
Booking
Book both days as soon as your flights are confirmed — weekend slots (your Day 6 falls on a Thursday, lower risk, but book early regardless) fill with local and tour-group demand.
Certification note: CERTAIN these are fun dives requiring an existing open-water certification for the actual dive slots — if either of you isn't certified, operators offer "discover scuba" intro dives instead (shallower, instructor-led 1:1), which changes group logistics slightly. Confirm certification status for everyone before booking so the operator quotes the right package.

06Practical Notes

TopicDetail
VisaCERTAIN Singapore passport holders get visa-free entry to Japan for short tourist stays (well within this trip's length). No action needed beyond a valid passport.
Flight schedule riskLIKELY this Sun/Tue/Wed/Sat pattern holds, but the route is barely a year old at booking time — recheck Scoot's schedule the moment it releases for March 2027, don't assume today's pattern is locked.
Getting around NahaYui Rail (monorail) covers the airport-to-city corridor and most in-town stops. Taxis for anything off the line.
Getting around North OkinawaRental car is the practical option for Day 5 and the Kouri/Manzamo leg — public transport up the Motobu peninsula is infrequent and slow. International driving permit required alongside your SG licence.
MoneyJapan still leans cash-heavy outside major chains. IC card (Suica-compatible) for the monorail; cash for markets and smaller eateries.
SIM/dataeSIM or airport SIM on arrival — needed for maps on the north-island day and coordinating dive-day pickup times.
Weather — MarchLIKELY Okinawa in March is cool-mild (high teens to low 20s°C air), calmer than typhoon season, but still winter-pattern seas — the reason the dive-day weather risk is called out above, not a hypothetical.
EmergencyJapan emergency: 110 (police), 119 (ambulance/fire). DAN (Divers Alert Network) contact worth having on file given two dive days.
