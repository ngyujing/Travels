03Camp Guides — Stay · Eat · Do

One established, one rawer — the mix you asked for, both inside the same district so the "relocate" costs you almost nothing.

Kota Tinggi Waterfall — Established

NIGHT 1 · MAY 20 · CAMPGROUND
Stay — ~RM5/pax campsite fee
Designated campsites near the main waterfall, on the same grounds as Kota Rainforest Resort. Toilets, showers, food stalls, parking on-site. Tent rental available if you want a backup. Resort desk: +60 19-755 0231 — call to confirm current camping arrangements and gate hours before you ride down.
Eat
Food stalls near the entrance; resort restaurant needs advance booking per reviews and runs pricey/slow — don't count on it for a casual dinner. Kota Tinggi town (15km) is the fallback for a proper meal.
Do
Main waterfall swim (24m drop, developed pool), secondary waterfall via a short trail, water slides if you're in the mood. Mind the monkeys on the approach road. Crowds build on weekends — you arrive on a Thursday, so this should be quiet.

Six Acres Campsite — Rawer

NIGHTS 2–3 · MAY 21–22 · HILLTOP + STREAM
Stay — rate unconfirmed
FLAGGED Hilltop plots (flat and sloped), shaded, natural stream, ~22–23°C at night. 4.2★ but only 13 reviews — one calls it "best in Johor," another reports host non-responsiveness on booking. Confirm directly and get it in writing before you ride down with no backup plan for the night.
Eat
No on-site food — pack in from Kota Tinggi town or the waterfall food stalls before you relocate. This is genuine self-sufficient camping for meals.
Do
Stream-side relaxation, short forest/hill trails, stargazing off the ridge, small playground if it matters to you. This is your actual rest day location — nothing scheduled here on purpose.
